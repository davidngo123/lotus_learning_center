Why Choose Individual Tutoring?

Our one-on-one tutoring program provides personalized attention and customized learning plans that adapt to your unique needs, schedule, and learning style.

  • Personalized Learning: Curriculum tailored to your specific goals and skill level
  • Flexible Scheduling: Choose times that work best for you
  • Individual Attention: 100% of your tutor's focus on your progress
  • Faster Progress: Learn at your own pace without group constraints
  • Cultural Understanding: Tutors sensitive to Vietnamese cultural background

How Individual Tutoring Works

A simple process from enrollment to achievement

1

Initial Assessment

We evaluate your current skill level and discuss your learning goals.

2

Tutor Matching

We pair you with a tutor who matches your needs and schedule.

3

Custom Plan

Your tutor creates a personalized learning plan just for you.

4

Track Progress

Regular check-ins and assessments to measure your advancement.

Flexible Scheduling

We work around your schedule to find times that work best for you.

  • Morning Sessions: 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM
  • Afternoon Sessions: 1:00 PM - 5:00 PM
  • Evening Sessions: 6:00 PM - 9:00 PM
  • Weekend Sessions: Saturday & Sunday available

Sessions can be in-person at our center or online via video call
