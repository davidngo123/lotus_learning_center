Our Story

Founded in 2018 by Vietnamese community members in Seattle, Lotus Learning Center emerged from a simple truth: access to education should never be a barrier to success.

Our founders, Mai Nguyen and Thanh Pham, recognized that many Vietnamese immigrants faced significant challenges in accessing quality educational resources. Language barriers, financial constraints, and unfamiliarity with American systems created obstacles that seemed insurmountable.

What started as informal tutoring sessions in a community center basement has grown into a comprehensive educational support network serving over 500 students annually. Our Mission

To empower Vietnamese immigrants in Seattle through accessible, culturally-responsive educational programs that foster language proficiency, workforce readiness, and community integration.

Our Vision

A thriving Vietnamese-American community in Seattle where every individual has the tools, confidence, and support to achieve their educational and professional aspirations.
