Volunteer Opportunities

Find the perfect role for your skills and interests

ESL Tutor

Most Needed

Teach English language skills to adult learners in small group or one-on-one settings.

  • Time: 2-4 hours/week
  • Experience: No teaching experience required
  • Training: Provided
  • Commitment: 3 months minimum

Translator/Interpreter

Vietnamese Speakers

Bridge language gaps by translating documents and interpreting during classes and events.

  • Time: Flexible, as needed
  • Experience: Fluent in Vietnamese & English
  • Training: Not required
  • Commitment: Flexible

Technology Instructor

Help students develop computer and digital literacy skills for work and daily life.

  • Time: 2-3 hours/week
  • Experience: Basic computer skills
  • Training: Curriculum provided
  • Commitment: 2 months minimum

Career Mentor

Guide students in job search, resume writing, interview prep, and career development.

  • Time: 1-2 hours/week
  • Experience: Professional work experience
  • Training: Orientation provided
  • Commitment: Flexible

Administrative Support

Assist with office tasks, event planning, registration, and program coordination.

  • Time: 3-5 hours/week
  • Experience: Office skills helpful
  • Training: On the job
  • Commitment: Flexible

Event Volunteer

Help organize and run community events, workshops, and cultural celebrations.

  • Time: Event-based (4-8 hours)
  • Experience: None required
  • Training: Brief orientation
  • Commitment: Single events or ongoing
