Our Mission

Lotus Learning Center is a grassroots community initiative dedicated to providing educational resources, English language support, and cultural integration assistance to Vietnamese immigrants in the greater Seattle area. We believe education is the foundation for opportunity and empowerment.

Learn More About Us

Our Programs

Comprehensive support for every stage of your educational journey

English Language Classes

Free ESL classes for adults at beginner, intermediate, and advanced levels. Learn English in a supportive, culturally-sensitive environment.

  • Small class sizes
  • Flexible schedules
  • Native Vietnamese-speaking tutors available

Family Literacy

Programs designed to help families learn together, supporting both children and parents in their educational journeys.

  • Parent-child learning activities
  • Homework help for students
  • Family reading programs

Workforce Development

Resume building, interview preparation, and job search assistance to help you navigate the American job market.

  • Career counseling
  • Professional skills training
  • Job placement assistance

Citizenship Preparation

Comprehensive support for the U.S. citizenship process, including test preparation and application assistance.

  • Civics test preparation
  • Interview practice
  • Application guidance

Digital Literacy

Learn essential computer skills, internet navigation, and digital tools for work and daily life.

  • Basic computer skills
  • Internet & email
  • Online safety

Community Support

Connect with resources, navigate social services, and build community connections in Seattle.

  • Resource referrals
  • Cultural events
  • Peer support groups
